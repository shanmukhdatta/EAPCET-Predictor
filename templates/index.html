How it works

Built straight from official allotment PDFs

  1. 01
    Source PDF

    The official AP EAPCET "Last Rank Details" notification is parsed table-cell by table-cell, preserving every column exactly as published.

  2. 02
    Structured database

    Every (college, branch, category, gender) closing rank becomes a typed, indexed row in SQLite — fast enough to query instantly.

  3. 03
    Your prediction

    We compare your rank against last year's closing ranks. A rank at or below the cutoff means a seat was available at that rank last year.

This is a guide, not a guarantee
